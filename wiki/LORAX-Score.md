# LORAX Score

  The LORAX (Level of Readiness, Alignment, and eXchange) score is a per-user engagement metric that influences NC/WC allocation, matchmaking, and platform privileges.

  ## Formula

  ```
  LORAX = I × D × W / 10,000
  ```

  Where:
  - **I** = Investment engagement (deal participation, deposits, circle activity)
  - **D** = Duration factor (time active on platform, consistency)
  - **W** = World impact (environmental category alignment, trust corpus contributions)

  ## How It's Used

  | System | LORAX Influence |
  |--------|----------------|
  | NC/WC Allocation | Higher LORAX = more favorable NC/WC split on transactions |
  | Matchmaking | LORAX proximity used in magnetic matchmaking |
  | Page Unlocking | Progressive feature access based on score |
  | Shopping Circles | Weighted influence in circle buying power calculation |
  | Banking Tier | Contributes to personal mesh cluster tier |

  ## LORAX-REMORA Coupling

  The LORAX score is coupled with the REMORA profiling system:
  - REMORA authenticity affects LORAX calculation
  - Catharsis detection triggers LORAX recalculation
  - Coupled oscillator feedback loops maintain equilibrium

  ## WorldState Refresh

  The LORAX system includes automatic worldState refresh:
  - Global economic indicators update periodically
  - NC/WC ratios adjust based on overall ecosystem health
  - Weather system reflects current economic conditions (CLEAR, STORM, etc.)

  ## Decay

  LORAX scores decay over time without activity:
  - Encourages continued engagement
  - Prevents inactive accounts from holding disproportionate influence
  - Decay rate is gradual and recoverable

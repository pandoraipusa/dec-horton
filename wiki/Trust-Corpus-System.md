# Trust Corpus System

  The Trust Corpus is the transparent fund management backbone of PandoraIP. Every dollar that flows through the platform is tracked, split, and recorded on the public ledger.

  ## 50-25-25 Syndicate Banking Model

  All funds are allocated according to the syndicate model:

  | Share | Allocation | Purpose |
  |-------|-----------|---------|
  | **50%** | Trust Corpus | Direct environmental investment — green energy, NGOs, sustainable ventures |
  | **25%** | Operations | Platform development, infrastructure, mesh network maintenance |
  | **25%** | Participant Returns | Returned to wallet holders as NC/WC credits and investment returns |

  ## DAI 2x Rule

  The Dual Allocation Index (DAI) 2x Rule governs contribution splitting:
  - Every contribution is doubled in accounting terms
  - 50% allocated to NC (Natural Capital)
  - 50% allocated to WC (World Capital)
  - This creates a balanced dual-currency flow that funds both environmental projects and operational sustainability

  ## Hierarchical Account System

  The Trust Corpus uses a hierarchical structure:

  ```
  Trust Corpus (Root)
  ├── Environmental Investment Fund
  │   ├── Green Energy Projects
  │   ├── NGO Funding
  │   └── Sustainable Ventures
  ├── Tax Credit Pool
  │   └── Automatic credits generated per transaction
  ├── Operations Fund
  │   ├── Infrastructure
  │   ├── Development
  │   └── Mesh Network
  └── Participant Returns
      ├── NC Credits
      ├── WC Credits
      └── Investment Yields
  ```

  ## Automatic Tax Credits

  Every transaction through the Trust Corpus automatically generates tax credits:
  - Credits are calculated based on the environmental impact category
  - Tracked per user in the Admin Tax Dashboard
  - Auditable through the Government Auditor Portal

  ## Public Ledger

  All Trust transactions are recorded on a transparent public ledger:
  - Viewable at pandoraip.live/public-ledger
  - Includes beneficial use tracking
  - Whistleblowing features for accountability
  - No transaction can be hidden or altered

  ## Donor Accountability

  The Donor Accountability System ensures transparent fund tracking:
  - Every donation is traced from source to deployment
  - Impact reports generated per project
  - Donors can verify their funds reached the intended purpose
